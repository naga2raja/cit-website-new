Our Software Maintenance Services

Our team of expert software professionals with diverse skills, a passion for the job, and over a decade's experience in the industry.
We provide you with effective long-term continual maintenance and real-time support services for all your software requirements.

Mainstream Services

Adaptive Maintenance

Necessary modifications and revisions to your software for the transformational needs of your business like application software maintenance - Data format change, Localization and regulation change, Hardware configuration change, Support Utility Modification, and Operating system integration.

Corrective Maintenance

Addresses the errors and faults within software applications that could impact various parts of software, including the design, logic, and code. These corrections usually come from bug reports that were created by users or customers – but corrective software maintenance can help to spot them before your customers do, which can help build your brand’s reputation.

Perfective Maintenance

Focuses on the evolution of requirements and features that existing in your system. From the enduser's perspective, suggestion of new features in the software, which could become future projects or enhancements. Perfective software maintenance takes over some of the work, both adding features that can enhance user experience and removing features that are not effective and functional.

Preventive Maintenance

Helps to make changes and adaptations to your software so that it can work for a longer period of time. The focus of the type of maintenance is to prevent the deterioration of your software as it continues to adapt and change. These services can include optimizing code and updating documentation as needed.
