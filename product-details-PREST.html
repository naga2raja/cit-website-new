PRest - Profitable Restraunt Mangement System

Problem

• Food wastage affects profitability of restaurants significantly.​

• Ineffective stock management leads to lot of food waste resulting in loss.



Solution

• PRest is a way of tracking what and how much inventory your restaurant is always carrying.

• Equipped with that information, restaurants can improve their inventory purchasing (i.e.,not buying too much or too little), minimize inventory lost due to theft, spoilage, or waste,and maximize profitability.

• PRest aims at reducing Food Wastages both on the table as well as on the stock.

• PRest lists out what menu can be prepared today based on the available stock and also mentions the quantity.
